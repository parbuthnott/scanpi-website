Scan.

ScanPI has successfully utilised both pic2shop and xzing scanning apps - available from all good app stores.

The returning barcode from those apps needs to be pointed to the bar2brainz2tracks.php function.

That function extracts the barcode and fires an API call at the music brainz website for an artist and album details and then looks that up on your local device to allow you to play it.
